Ive been COOKING Steaks WRONG all this time? WHAT!?

Today we try a steak on a shovel. This was done a long time ago and today we put it to the test.

Prep: 15 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Total: 30 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

Wagyu A5 Ribeye

Salt

Pepper

Garlic Powder

Directions

Step 1

Start by making sure you are using a CARBON STEEL shovel, then scrub it well with soap and water and then sterilize the shovel over a hot fire.

Step 2

Season your steaks with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Lightly spread a even layer of oil onto your shovel.

Step 3

Now over open flame on your grill get your shovel nice and hot and place your steak on it. let it get a nice crust on both side and cook until it reaches and internal temperature of 135°F.

Step 4

Now all that's left is to slice up your steaks and enjoy your 1800'S SHOVEL STEAKS!
