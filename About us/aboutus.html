ABOUT GUGA

Welcome to Gugas Website! Here you will find anything and everything Guga has ever made! Guga has three YouTube Channels, and short content on Instagram, Facebook TikTok and Snapchat where he takes you on a culinary journey like no other.

In his first YouTube Channel Guga Foods, the love for barbecue is real! Guga will experiment with all sorts of protein including his favorite; STEAKS! He will show you the best and worst ways to cook from the cheapest cuts to the most luxurious foods. In SousVideEverything he will do exactly that! Sous vide everything he can think of and show you the best way to cook using the sous vide technique. His most recent channel Guga is simply that, a combination of everything that encompasses Guga on a personal level. He creates a combination of short and long format videos with everything related to food, restaurants, lifestyle, vlogs, podcast. Here everything goes!

In his videos Guga showcases his successes and failures, allowing the audience to learn from his mistakes. And there are plenty of those! But he has learned that without taking those risks he would have never discovered new amazing food combinations. Guga will take you along detailed recipes and will give tips and tricks for the average home cook to enhance their cooking skills and demonstrate that there is no reason to be intimidated by the kitchen.

Come along the for journey and stay for the STEAKS!
